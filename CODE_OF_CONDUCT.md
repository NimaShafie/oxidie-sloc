# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want oxide-sloc to be a respectful, constructive project.

Participants are expected to:
- be respectful and professional
- assume good intent while giving direct technical feedback
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and process, not people
- avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks
- respect differing levels of experience

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- harassment or intimidation
- hateful or discriminatory language
- doxxing or unwanted disclosure of private information
- repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews
- abusive or insulting conduct

## Scope

This applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other official communication channels for oxide-sloc.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or block participants whose behavior harms the project.
